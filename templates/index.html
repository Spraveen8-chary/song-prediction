How it works

Emotion Inference

The system analyzes user input to gauge its sentiment and extract keywords. These are compared against predefined emotional themes to predict the dominant emotional tone.

Theme-Based Music

Using the predicted emotion, the system selects songs associated with the identified emotional theme from a dataset. A subset of diverse songs is randomly chosen to offer tailored music options.

Personalized Experience

Users receive the predicted emotional theme and a selection of songs that match their sentiment. This seamless process ensures users enjoy a curated musical journey that aligns with their emotions.
